ABSTRAK

Di dalam SMP Santa Ursula Jakarta, terdapat salah satu mata pelajaran yang penting yaitu, Integrated Learning. Mata pelajaran ini difokuskan untuk meningkatkan tingkat kinerja dalam bidang kewirausahaan, perdagangan, dan sifat kerjasama antar anggota. Integrated Learning kelas IX SMP Santa Ursula Jakarta memiliki kegiatan dan tugas untuk mendirikan stan bazar. Proyek besar tersebut dilakukan pada tanggal 5 Desember 2023, tepatnya pada saat istirahat pertama dan kedua. Target konsumen yang ditemukan adalah siswa dan siswi SD, SMP, dan SMA Santa Ursula Jakarta. Penjualan tidak hanya diadakan pada saat hari-h secara offline, namun dibuka juga penjualan secara online dengan sistem pre-order.

Perencanaan merupakan salah satu bagian penting di dalam kegiatan bazar ini, sebab tanpa perencanaan tidak akan ada eksekusi penjualan yang maksimal. Perencanaan yang dilakukan sebelum hari pelaksanaan tiba tentunya banyak sekali, termasuk perencanaan nama toko, logo, produk jual, harga jual, tema, dekorasi, dan juga promosi. Ketika hari pelaksanaan tiba, seluruh anggota kelompok saling membagi tugas dan bekerja dalam bidang masing-masing, sehingga penjualan dapat berjalan dengan lancar. Ketika pelaksanaan telah selesai, kami tetap saling bekerja sama untuk menghitung pendapatan, mendata semua produk yang terjual, merapikan dekorasi booth, dan membersihkan sisa-sisa sampah dan dekorasi.

Keseluruhan rangkaian proses perencanaan dan pelaksanaan berjalan dengan lancar. Proses perencanaan dan pelaksanaan telah memberikan banyak sekali ilmu bermanfaat bagi kami. Di dalam segi berkewirausahaan, mengelola keuangan, pembagian tugas, dan kerjasama antar anggota. Pelaksanaan Integrated Learning ini juga memberikan manfaat untuk proses ujian praktek seluruh siswi kelas IX dikarenakan dengan hadirnya program ini, maka keseluruhan ujian praktek dapat dilaksanakan menggunakan bahan Integrated Learning tersebut.


ABSTRACT

Integrated Learning is a crucial subject in SMP Santa Ursula Jakarta. It aims to enhance students' performance in entrepreneurship, selling skills, and teamwork. In this subject, grade IX students were tasked with setting up a bazaar booth. The project was carried out on December 5, 2023, during the first and second breaks. The target market was targeted at students from elementary, middle, and high schools in Saint Ursula Jakarta. The products were sold offline during the event and online through a pre-order system.

Planning was considered as a crucial part of the bazaar. Without proper planning, the sales execution would not have been optimized. The planning process included brainstorming for the store name, logo, products, prices, theme, decoration, and promotion. During the planning and execution, the group were faced various challenges and some successes were achieved. From the beginning, It was realized that every member's opinion and contribution was crucial and had a significant impact on the group's progress. Therefore, whenever there were disagreements, a discussion would be held by the group and the option with the majority vote would be chosen. During the execution, some miscommunication issues were experienced by the group, which slightly hindered the sales process. However, the group decided to improve their communication, leading to a smoother sales process and customer satisfaction.

The entire planning and implementation process was carried out smoothly. The planning and implementation process has provided us with a lot of useful knowledge in terms of entrepreneurship, financial management, task division, and cooperation between members. The implementation of Integrated Learning also has provided benefits for the practical exam process for all class IX students because with the presence of this program, the entire practical exam can be carried out using the Integrated Learning materials.
